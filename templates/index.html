About BIWC Kwabenya

Baptist International Worship Centre is a vibrant, spirit-filled congregation committed to worshiping God with excellence, reaching the lost with compassion, and developing disciples of Jesus Christ. Located in the heart of Kwabenya, we welcome everyone seeking a deeper relationship with God.

  • Spirit-Filled Worship - Engaging and dynamic services
  • Biblical Teaching - Grounded in God's Word
  • Community Service - Reaching our neighborhood
  • Discipleship - Growing disciples of Christ
